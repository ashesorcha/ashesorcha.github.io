Secret Tomato Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 yellow onion, cut in half
  • 2 cups chopped tomatoes
  • 8 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Optional Additions:

  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil
  • Bread

Preparation:

  1. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat, then add the chopped tomatoes and the halved onion, cut side down.
  2. Bring the tomato sauce to a simmer, then add a drizzle of olive oil, followed by the salt and pepper.
  3. Simmer over a low heat, stirring occasionally, for around 45 minutes, or until the oil seperates from the sauce.
  4. To Serve: Serve the tomato sauce over pasta, topped with grated parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
