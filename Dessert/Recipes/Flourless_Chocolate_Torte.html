Flourless Chocolate Torte

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons water
  • A pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 1/2 ounces bittersweet chocolate
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 eggs, at room temperature

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees Farenheight. Grease one small 6 inch diameter cake pan or other small baking pan and set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the water, salt, and sugar. Stir over medium heat until completely dissolved and set aside.
  3. Bring some water to a boil in a large saucepan. Place another smaller saucepan into the boiling water and slowly melt the chocolate, stirring constantly to prevent burning. When fully melted, pour the chocolate into a large bowl.
  4. Cut the butter into cubes and beat the butter into the melted chocolate, one piece at a time. Beat in the hot sugar-water mixture. Then, slowly beat in the eggs, one at a time.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Put the pan into a larger pan filled halfway up the sides of the cake pan with boiling water.
  6. Bake the cake in the water bath in the oven for about 20 minutes. The center will still look wet, but the batter will have set slightly. Chill the cake overnight in the pan. To unmold, dip the bottom of the cake pan in hot water for around 10 seconds and invert onto a serving plate.
  7. Serve cold with berries, whipped cream, or raspberry coullis.
