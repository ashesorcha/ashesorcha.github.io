Baccala Alla Cappuccina

Ingredients:

Serves 4 people

  • 4 Fresh Codfish Fillets (about 150 grams each)
  • 1 large sweet onion, julienned
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pine nuts
  • 1 tablespoon raisins
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • A handful of flour
  • A handful of breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation:

Recipe source: "Baccalà Alla Cappuccina", Venice, Food and Wine Lineadacqua

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit, or 180 degrees Celcius.
  2. In an oven pan, saute the onions in the olive oil over a moderate flame until transparent.
  3. Dredge the fillet lightly in flour, then add them to the pan and cook on both sides until golden - a minute or two.
  4. Now add the vinegar, wine, pine nuts, raisins, sugar, cinnamon stick, salt, and pepper. Move these around gently for another minute or two, without turning or breaking the fish, until they are mixed together.
  5. Sprinkle the breadcrumbs evenly over the mixture and place in the oven. Bake, uncovered, for around 10 minutes or until the breadcrumb topping is crispy and golden brown.
