Carrot and Almond Torte

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs, yolks and whites separated
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 pound carrots, finely grated
  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1 heaped tablespoon self-rising flour

To Serve:

  • Confectioners' sugar, for dusting
  • Crème fraîche or whipped cream

Preparation:

Recipe source: Crapanzano, Aleksandra, "Carrot and Almond Torte", Wall Street Journal, New York, N.Y, 09 Feb 2016

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celcius), and line an 8X8 cake pan with parchement paper.
  2. Combine the egg yolks, sugar, orange zest, and vanilla in a large bowl and beat for about 5 minutes, or until very pale, thick, and creamy.
  3. Mix in the grated carrot, almond flour, and self-rising flour until fully and evenly combined. Then, in a separate bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  4. Incorporate 1/3 of the egg whites into the batter. Then, when the mixture is slightly loosened, add the remaining 2/3 and very gently fold in the egg whites, being careful to knock as little air out as possible, just until white streaks are no longer visible.
  5. Pour and spread the batter evenly into the lined pan and place in the oven for about 35-45 minutes, or until a knife inserted into the center of the dish comes out clean, and a bubbly golden-brown top has formed.
  6. Let the cake cool in the pan for 10-15 minutes before serving. To serve, dust the top with a bit of powdered sugar and serve alongside a dollop of crème fraîche or whipped cream and berries.
