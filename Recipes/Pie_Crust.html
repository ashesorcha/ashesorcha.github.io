Pie Crust

Ingredients:

Makes 1 9in pie crust

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/3 cup lard (refrigerated)
  • 2 tablespoons cold butter, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup ice water

Preparation:

  1. In a food processor, combine the flour and salt and pulse a few times to mix.
  2. Break apart the cold lard using your hands and add the chunks to the flour mixture along with the cubes of butter. Pulse these elements together until the mixture looks like sand. Be very careful not to over-mix; just a few pulses will do.
  3. While the food processor is on and spinning, slowly pour in the ice-cold water. Mix just until the contents of the mixer forms one ball.
  4. Remove the dough from the food processor, roll it into a ball, flatten it, and cover it in plastic-wrap. Refrigerate the dough for 1 to 1 1/2 hours, or until firm, before working into a pastry.
