Cinnamon-Raisin Granola

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1 cup quick-cooking oatmeal
  • 1 cup rice krispies
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon almond extract

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit, or 175 degrees Celcius.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the old-fashioned oats, oatmeal, rice krispies, raisins, and cinnamon.
  3. Bring the honey, maple syrup, and brown sugar to a simmer in a small saucepan, stirring constantly to combine. Once simmering, turn off the heat and add the vanilla and almond extracts. Pour the syrup mixture over the dry ingredients and stir until sticky and well-coated.
  4. Pour the contents of the large bowl onto a parchment-lined baking sheet. Spread the mixture evenly out on the pan.
  5. Place the pan into the oven and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the top of the granola has turned golden brown. At this point, take the granola out of the oven and let it cool completely on the pan before storing it in a jar.
  6. Serve on top of yogurt, or with milk, accompanied by fresh berries, nuts, or dried fruit of your choosing.
