Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup (one 8 ounce stick) butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/3 cups, or about 6 mashed overripe bananas

Optional Additions:

  • Coarsely-chopped nuts (walnuts, hazelnuts, pecans, etc...)
  • Dried fruit
  • Chocolate chips
  • ...

Preparation:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (175 degrees Celsius), and lightly grease a 9X5 inch loaf pan with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir the dry ingredients together until well and evenly mixed.
  3. In a seperate bowl, cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and then the mashed bananas until well blended. Now pour the banana mixture into the flour mixture. Stir the wet and dry ingredients together just to combine - overmixing will make the bread too dense.
  4. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and bake in the oven for about an hour, or until the top is cracked, brown, and a wooden skewer inserted into the middle of the loaf comes out clean. Let the bread cool in the pan for about 5 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
  5. To serve, slice and present immediately when warm, or let the loaf cool completely and drizzle with a glaze made of icing sugar, water, and a flavoring of your choice. This bread is also great toasted and served with butter. Suggested pairings: Caramel, cherry, chocolate, cinnamon, coffee, ginger, hazelnut, honey, lime, molasses, fresh berries, cream, etc...
